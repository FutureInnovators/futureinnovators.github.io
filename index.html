  • Innovative Pitch Competition

    April 2018

    Young innovators from across the state of Indiana will come together this spring in Indianapolis for a statewide pitch competition. This opportunity, produced by the Indiana Economic Development Corporation and Ball State University, supports the belief that innovation and entrepreneurship drive the state forward and sustain economic success. In order to reach this success, we know that creating, supporting and promoting opportunities for innovation will drive that progress for the state of Indiana toward a more sustainable future. Through the Future Innovators of Indiana Pitch Competition, we aim to create an opportunity for high school students to expand their education and share their passions. Empowering these young minds to make their visions tangible encourages the best vision of Indiana to become a reality.

  • What is the Future Innovators of Indiana Pitch Competition?

    This competition is an opportunity for high school students to bring their innovative ideas to life. Participants will receive feedback from experienced professionals while competing with like-minded individuals from around the state of Indiana.

    After online submissions, many young innovators will be selected to come to Indianapolis to present their pitch to a live panel of judges for a chance to win valuable prizes.

    The submission deadline is
    February 10, 2018.
