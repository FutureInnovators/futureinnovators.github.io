  • Idea Pitch Competition

    April 2018

    Join fellow young innovators from across Indiana as they come together this spring in Indianapolis for a statewide pitch competition! This opportunity, produced by the Indiana Economic Development Corporation and Ball State University, supports the belief that innovation and entrepreneurship drive the state forward and sustain economic success. To reach this success, we are committed to creating, supporting and promoting opportunities for innovation. Through the Innovate WithIN Pitch Competition, we aim to create an opportunity for high school students to expand their education and share their passions.

  • What is the Innovate WithIN Pitch Competition?

    This competition is an opportunity for high school students to bring their innovative ideas to life. Participants will receive feedback from experienced professionals while competing against like-minded individuals from around the state.

    After online submissions, one innovator or team from each region will be invited to come to Indianapolis to pitch to a panel of judges for a chance to win incredible prizes.
